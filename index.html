What is the CM Punjab Asaan Karobar Card?

The Asaan Karobar Card is a financial initiative by the Government of Punjab offering interest-free business loans of up to PKR 1 million. It aims to support small entrepreneurs by providing them with a flexible revolving credit facility.

Eligibility Criteria

To apply, ensure you meet the following requirements:

  • Age: 21-57 years old
  • Resident of Punjab, Pakistan
  • Valid CNIC
  • Registered mobile number
  • Existing business or a new business plan
  • Clean credit history

How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply:

  1. Visit the official portal: Asaan Karobar Card Login
  2. Register with your CNIC and mobile number
  3. Fill in personal and business details
  4. Upload required documents
  5. Pay the PKR 500 processing fee
  6. Submit the application
